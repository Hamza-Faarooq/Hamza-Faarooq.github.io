Research Intern · University of Manchester · Prof. Anirbit Mukherjee

Engineered a scalable Deep Operator Network (DeepONet) to solve the spatiotemporal Burgers PDE, achieving a minimum MSE loss of 0.0055. Developed comprehensive visualization pipelines and optimized data processing across 819K+ points, ensuring robust neural operator training across multiple initial conditions.

Sparsity-Aware Optimization for Spiking Neural Networks
Undergraduate Researcher · University of Oulu · Prof. Shuai Lee
Mar 2025 – Jun 2025
Formulated a constrained quadratic optimization approach to prune Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs), achieving 87.5% spatial sparsity. Integrated criticality-based neuron selection and KL-divergence temporal regularization to jointly reduce spatial and temporal redundancy, benchmarking against standard ANN baselines.

Multimodal Fusion for Movie Review Helpfulness Prediction
Undergraduate Researcher · IIM Ranchi · Prof. Sobhan Sarkar
May 2025 – Aug 2025
