Physics-Informed Generative CFD Solver

2025
PyTorch Neural Operators DDPM PIKAN-FNO
Navier-Stokes PDEs U-Net backbone Physics-constrained

Designed a physics-informed generative framework (PIKAN-FNO) integrating Kolmogorov Arnold Networks with Fourier Neural Operators to solve steady-state Navier-Stokes equations with high-fidelity approximations.

  • Developed a Denoising Diffusion Probabilistic Model (DDPM) with a custom U-Net backbone to generate continuous fluid flow fields, enabling data-driven initialization for physics-constrained refinement.
  • Addressed training instability in PINNs by formulating an Ansatz for exact boundary condition enforcement and implementing dynamic loss-weighting to balance PDE residuals and constraint terms.
  • Validated model performance through quantitative error analysis and qualitative flow-field visualization, demonstrating consistency with governing fluid dynamics.

Robustness & Calibration Analysis of Transformer Classifiers

2025
PyTorch Transformers DistilBERT Calibration
67k-sample SST-2 38% relative ECE increase ECE 0.074→0.103

Fine-tuned DistilBERT on 67k-sample SST-2 dataset, achieving 90% validation accuracy and evaluated robustness under prompt and token-level perturbations, observing 38% relative ECE increase despite accuracy degradation.

  • Performed reliability analysis using ECE (0.074 → 0.103) and reliability diagrams, revealing systematic overconfidence and confidence–accuracy misalignment of softmax outputs under simulated distribution shifts.
  • Benchmarked against standard baselines to isolate calibration drift from accuracy degradation under out-of-distribution prompts and adversarial token-level perturbations.
