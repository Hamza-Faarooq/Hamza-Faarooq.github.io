Sparsity-Aware Optimization for Spiking Neural Networks
Undergraduate Researcher · University of Oulu · Prof. Shuai Lee
Mar 2025 – Jun 2025

Formulated a constrained quadratic optimization approach to prune Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs), achieving 87.5% spatial sparsity. Integrated criticality-based neuron selection and KL-divergence temporal regularization to jointly reduce spatial and temporal redundancy, benchmarking against ANN baselines.

Projects

ML-Driven Volatility Surface Modeling for High-Frequency NIFTY50 Options

Engineered a high-frequency options volatility forecasting pipeline on 578,000+ NIFTY50 records with 100+ engineered features. Achieved ultra-low MSE of 0.00009 using a batch-wise ensemble model with strike-aware feature selection — outperforming standard baselines by over 10×.

Handwritten Digit Recognition — Neural Network from Scratch

Achieved 91.8% test accuracy and minimum loss of 0.007 with a fully custom-built, modular feedforward neural network — zero reliance on ML frameworks. Engineered and optimized the complete training pipeline including backprop, activations, and weight initialization.
